Details

● The objectives of this game

For this project, we had to think about a serious game centered around the Jardin du Peyrou, a park located in the city of Montpellier, France. We decided to show the player the significant historical stages of this park. So, we came up with the idea of allowing the player to time-travel, landing them at moments marking significant changes in the park.

● Time winding system

Date change

The player can simply increase or decrease the date by pressing the up or down arrows. They can then travel to the chosen date.

The available dates are stored in an array that is saved to allow the player to exit the game without concern.

● Mini Games

Magic coloring

For this mini-game, the player has to color the boxes with the corresponding colors. Each color is associated with a date.

A raycast is sent from the player's camera to the point where the player clicked. A detection box is hidden behind each part of the drawing to receive this raycast.

Once the coloring is complete with the correct colors, the player wins the mini-game !

Hammer game

For this mini-game, the player must unlock the stone blocking the water flow.

To do this, they need to press the screen when the cursor is aligned with the green bar.

The green bar decreases with each successful hit. Succeed three times to win the mini-game !

The fall

For this mini-game, the player must help the revolutionaries topple the statue.

To do so, they need to quickly press the screen to increase the gauge.

Once the gauge is full, you win the mini-game !
