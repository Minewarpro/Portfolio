Details

This game was made in a 48-hour game jam with the theme: "The fifth element", and ours is Alcohol !

The principle is simple: reach the end of the street to get to the bar faster than your opponent. You can drink on the way to go faster. But don't drink too much or you might end up not being able to walk straight !

Anything goes: hitting, throwing, and even... vomiting ?!
