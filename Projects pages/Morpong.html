Morpong

Software Used Unreal
Languages Used Blueprint

Details

This game was made in a 48-hour game jam with the theme: "Roots"

Our idea was to go back to the roots of video games and create a mashup between tic-tac-toe (OXO, the first video game for some) and Pong, a classic game.

So, you'll face off against your opponent locally to play a classic game of tic-tac-toe but with a ball that you'll need to return accurately to your opponent.
