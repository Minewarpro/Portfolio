Details

For this project, I aimed to create a Casual Game. To achieve this, it requires a simple and effective concept. During the development of this game, I tried to incorporate as much satisfying feedback as possible to engage the player.

● Règles du jeu

Classic Mode

The rules are simple: Slide the cube to the correct-colored square as quickly as possible.

You'll have 30 seconds to score as many points as possible.

Try to beat your high score each time you play !

Hard Mode

The rules are almost the same, the colors are just more difficult to distinguish from each other.

A challenge for the most seasoned players.

● Make easy fun

For this game, I aimed to create instant fun by providing the player with as much dopamine as possible. That's why it's essential to minimize player frustration as much as possible.

I also added visually pleasing effects to watch, always with the aim of providing dopamine to the player.

A pleasant touch

Given that this game is designed for mobile users, it imposes certain constraints. For example, the surface of the phone that the user is utilizing is special, as seen in the image below. Therefore, we had to think about how to launch the cube.

The GIF on the right shows that when the cube is launched diagonally, it returns in a vertical or horizontal direction to assist the player and avoid frustration.

Visual Effects

It's important to reward the player every time they perform a good action. That's why the visual effects appear with each point earned.

However, when the player misses their shot, visual effects and vibrations are also present to help them understand their mistake.
