About me

Gameplay Programmer

Hi ! My name is Hugo, I'm a Gameplay Programmer from France. I'm passionate about the world of video games. I've been immersed in this field since my earliest childhood. So it's only natural that I should be interested in creating these games. I'm currently studying at ESMA Game to learn the profession of gameplay programmer.

Below you'll find some of my personal and group projects. I love seeing people play the games I've created, and that's why I invest the time and passion necessary to give them the best possible gaming experience. I may be a programmer, but I attach a lot of importance to the creative side of video games. That's why I take care to polish the games I work on as best I can.

Recent Projects

Unchained

Unreal 5 PC Node Group

I found this one very interesting because I had to create a new mechanic based on physics. The player is attached to a ball and chain. The entire gameplay is based on this. It was my first group project.

"You wake up locked in a cave, tied to a ball and chain. It could be your best friend or your worst enemy. It's up to you to use it skillfully to solve the puzzles and regain your freedom."

Ylmo

Phaser Web PC JavaScript

This game is played directly on a web browser. It's a 2d platformer with an Ori-inspired atmosphere. It's my first finished and polished video game.

"You play a little will-o'-the-wisp and have to escape the dragon to save your life! This platformer is, for the most part, a runner."
