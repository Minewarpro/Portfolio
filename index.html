About me

Technical Game Designer

Hi ! My name is Hugo, I'm a new developer from France. I'm currently working as a Technical Game Designer at Osome Studio in Lyon.

I have a strong affinity for prototyping. It’s where I can fully express my creativity and share it with the entire team. To me, it’s one of the most effective ways to work collaboratively.

Video games have the power to deliver intense emotions to players. But to achieve that, the experience must be crafted as a whole, with input from every discipline. That’s what drives me every day: working as a team to create meaningful, memorable experiences for players.
