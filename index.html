About me

Gameplay Programmer

Hi ! My name is Hugo, I'm a Gameplay Programmer from french. I'm passionate about the world of video games. I've been immersed in this field since my earliest childhood. So it's only natural that I should be interested in creating these games. I'm currently studying at ESMA Game to learn the profession of gameplay programmer.

Below you'll find some of my personal and group projects. I love seeing people play the games I've created, and that's why I invest the time and passion necessary to give them the best possible gaming experience. I may be a programmer, but I attach a lot of importance to the creative side of video games. That's why I take care to polish the games I work on as best I can.
